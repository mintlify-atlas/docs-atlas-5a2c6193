title: "Submitting icons"
description: "Request new icons or submit updates to the Dashboard Icons collection"
---

There are two ways to submit icons to Dashboard Icons: the web form at dashboardicons.com (preferred) and GitHub Issues (slower). Use the web form whenever possible.

## Preferred method: dashboardicons.com

<Tip>This is the fastest path — admins can approve and publish your icon in minutes after submission.</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit dashboardicons.com">
    Go to [dashboardicons.com](https://dashboardicons.com) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the submission form">
    Fill in the service name, upload your SVG file, and add light or dark variants if the icon requires them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for review">
    Submit the form. Admins and Homarr Labs contributors review submissions directly through the site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Icon is published">
    Once approved, your icon is published immediately and available across the collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Alternative method: GitHub Issues

<Warning>GitHub submissions may take significantly longer to process. The issue templates for icon submission are being migrated — the dashboardicons.com form is the preferred channel for all new contributions.</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check for existing issues">
    Search [open issues](https://github.com/homarr-labs/dashboard-icons/issues) before submitting to avoid duplicates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the issue template">
    Open a [new issue](https://github.com/homarr-labs/dashboard-icons/issues/new/choose) and choose the template that matches your contribution — **Light & dark icon** or **Normal icon**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide service details">
    Include the service or application name, a link to the official logo source, and any specific requirements or notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach the SVG file (optional)">
    You can upload the SVG file directly to the issue. Include both light and dark variants if applicable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for team review">
    The team will review your request, request adjustments if needed, and add the icon to the collection when approved.
  </Step>
</Steps>
